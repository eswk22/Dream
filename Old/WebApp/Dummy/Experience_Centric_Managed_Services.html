Experience Centric Managed Services (ECMS)

Executive Summary

A key differentiator for Communications Service Providers (CSP) can be to deliver enhanced customer experience. As a result, CSPs increasingly focus Customer Experience Management.
Ericsson Managed Services approach to Customer Experience Management involves the evolution of capabilities within Network Operations Center (NOC) to Service Operations Center (SOC) and eventually to an Experience Management Center (EMC). With these capabilities we offer Experience Centric Managed Services helping CSPs to manage end-user customer experience.
