Operate Services Wireline Offerings

Wireline operators are facing significant challenges as they evolve their networks and services while operating a hybrid environment. To name a few of these challenges, they are migrating legacy TDM networks to IP networks. In parallel they are expanding fiber networks or finding alternatives through fixed wireless solutions. They are also increasing network capacity (40G to 100G) while trying to understand how network and service layers will be impacted by virtualization.

RNAM Operate Services has a tailored wireline service offerings backed by strong proof points.
